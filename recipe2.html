Beef Stew & Chapati

  • Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 500g beef, chopped
  • 2 onions, 3 tomatoes
  • Garlic, ginger, spices
  • Chapati flour, water, salt, oil

Instructions

  1. Boil and fry beef with onions, garlic, tomatoes, and spices.
  2. Mix flour, water, and salt for dough. Roll and cook chapatis.
  3. Serve the stew with fresh chapatis.
