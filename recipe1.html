Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bunch Sukuma Wiki (collard greens)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt & oil

Instructions

  1. Boil water, add maize flour gradually, stir until thick.
  2. Cook Sukuma with onions and tomatoes until tender.
  3. Serve hot with the Ugali.
